Our Team!

For this project our goal is to create a informative website that informs the user of availability of game stations, work machines, and events happening at a gaming caf√©/ gaming computer lab/ gaming venue. In this case we will be using the CSUN Gaming Room as reference. The website/web application will give the user information of the session times for active sessions on stations as well as the status of the machine. (e.g. Unavailable, in use, available). In addition the site will give users a general information bulletin containing procedures, rules, and upcoming events at the Gaming Room using a calendar. Our website will allow the general users to save time by knowing whats going on at the location to determine whether they want to spend their gap times between classes to go play some games or go elsewhere on campus.
